Studio Space

They say school is an extension of a home. The space that we call our school, our abode, where we learn, grow, share, is a space we respect immensely. It is where we offer our students an opportunity to immerse themselves in the divine art. It's a sacred space where we persevere to preserve our culture and our art. It's our safe haven, a space we consider to be an extension of our home. 

We open this space and share our sanctum with like minded people who need a place to practice their art, collaborate with fellow artists and creators or for any endeavor they may undertake to preserve the traditional art of dance and music.

Looking for a space?

If you are looking for a space to rehearse, practice your craft or conduct small events related to fine arts, please reach out to us. Write to our email address or simply fill the form below with your needs and we will get back to you.
