About our school


NLD was founded in October 2010 and follows the Thanjavur & Vazhuvoor style of Bharatanatyam. At NLD, we take pride in preserving the traditional roots of this divine art. We are strong believers of the Guru-Shishya Parampara and strive to preserve the authenticity of our art and culture. The core techniques and values of this art are imparted to eager learners in a focused setting. Every student and parent makes NLD what it is today and I am ever grateful to the Lord, my Gurus and my well-wishers for this blessing. Here’s a glimpse of NLD’s journey over the past 15 years.

classes at our school

In person classes are conducted at our dance studio in Dublin, CA. Kids aged 5 & above are eligible for enrolment. We accept students in the beginner, intermediate & advanced levels, after a thorough assessment of the skill level of the interested candidate. The primary teacher is Smt. Sudha Krishnan but we also have advanced level students who are trained to be a Teacher’s Assistant when needed. We also have adult-student batches.
