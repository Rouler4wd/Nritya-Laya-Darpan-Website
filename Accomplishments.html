Accomplishments

Pictures speak a 1000 words and that’s exactly what the one above does ! Nritya Laya Darpan started out as a dance school but, in time, it branched out with several other avenues to explore and learn this traditional form of dance. It stands as the root for several offshoots that branch off for the benefit of not only its students, but also for all aspirants in this art form - Bharatanatyam. We look forward to bringing ventures that open up avenues for larger audiences through collaborations to unify like minded and passionate seekers. Darpan Gazette

In May 2020, during the period of lockdown, we started an in-house newsletter that saw some interesting chronicles, edited and published on our internal google site by an editorial team of school students. They have almost 5 editions and 2 videos - a Candid Talk with our school’s founder and a video montage that encapsulates the journey of a decade to celebrate our school’s Decennary year during October 2020. The newsletters can be accessed here - The Darpan Gazette. Here is a video montage of accomplishments at NLD in 2020 - https://youtu.be/BMs-MuLBN_U

Nritya Sudharnava ~ Nectarine Ocean of Dance

Was inaugurated under the esteemed presence of Dr. Sridhar Vasudevan. The maiden performance of the series was by NLD’s student Kum. Varsha Punati. The second showcase was on Jan 2nd, 2021 by Kum. Shriya Krishnan. Nritya Sudharnava has merged with Darpana Reflector Series as part of its performance platform which serves as a medium for aspiring and budding artists to showcase their passion for any Indian classical art form. We encourage fellow dance schools and their students to utilize this space and join us to showcase the many upcoming talents. We also welcome accomplished artists to this platform, to perform, inspire and share their vast knowledge with the budding artists. Go to this page or visit www.darpanaseries.org for details.
